Home Remedies For Acidity

What is Acidity?

Acid is naturally secreted by the stomach and is necessary for digestion. During digestion, this acid aids in the breakdown of food. Acidity is a disorder that occurs when the stomach's gastric glands produce an excessive amount of acid.

Some of the symptoms include dyspepsia, heartburn, and ulcer formation. It is more common in people who are intensely emotional or worried.

Common Causes of Acidity

  • Consumption of alcohol
  • Highly spicy foodstuffs
  • Non-vegetarian diets
  • Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAID's)

Most acidity problems occur

  • After meals
  • When lifting weights or exerting tension and pressure in the abdomen area.
  • When lying down at night.

Ulcers can also be caused by excessive acid secretion.
