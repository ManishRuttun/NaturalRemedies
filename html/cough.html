What is Cough?

Coughing is a reflex that occurs when mucus, phlegm, dust, smoke, or a foreign body clogs the airways. The brain misinterprets an obstruction or irritation in the throat or upper airways as a foreign item and attempts to relieve it by coughing. If you've been coughing for more than two weeks, you should see a doctor.

Common Causes of Cough

  • Viral infection
  • Common cold
  • Flu
  • Smoking

Other serious causes may include:

  • Asthma
  • Bronchitis
  • Pneumonia
