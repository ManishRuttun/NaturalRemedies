Home Remedies For Asthma

What is Asthma?

Asthma is a chronic lung illness marked by a reduction in one's capacity to breathe easily. The airways that deliver air to the air sacs deep inside the lungs are clogged, preventing air from flowing in and out. Asthma develops when the bigger airways (bronchi) branch into smaller airways (bronchioles), which can be clogged by muscular tightness, inflammation or edema in the airways, and mucus collection. Asthma can be acute or chronic in nature. Coughing, wheezing, shortness of breath, and chest tightness are all symptoms of asthma.

Common Causes of Asthma

  • Allergy to pollen, dust mites
  • Air pollution
  • Respiratory infections
  • Non-specific hyperirritability
  • Sulfites in food
  • Certain medications
