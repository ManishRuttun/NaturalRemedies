Home Remedies For Acne / Pimples

What is Acne?

Acne is a skin ailment characterized by red, inflamed pimples and non-inflamed pimples. Hormones induce the skin's oil glands to expand, blocking the gland as well as the hair follicle to which it is linked. These pimples are most commonly found on the face, although they can also be found on the chest and neck. Acne usually clears up without leaving any scars.

Common Causes of Acne

  • An excessive amount of oil is secreted by the skin.
  • Changes in hormones during puberty
  • Oily secretions build up on the skin's surface.
  • Blockage of the skin's exterior pores
  • Hereditary
  • Menstrual periods
  • Pregnancy
  • Contraceptive pills
  • Stress
